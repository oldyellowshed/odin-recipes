Meatloaf

Meatloaf is a savory dish made from ground meat, beef, mixed with a variety of ingredients such as breadcrumbs, eggs, onions, garlic, and seasonings.

The mixture is shaped into a loaf and baked until cooked through. The meatloaf's texture is firm yet tender, with a slightly dense but moist consistency.

Ingredients

  • For the loaf:
    • Ground beef
    • Eggs
    • Onion
    • Milk
    • Bread crumbs
    • Salt
    • Pepper
  • For the sauce:
    • Ketchup
    • Brown sugar
    • Mustard

Steps

  1. Mix the loaf ingredients, then transfer to a loaf pan.
  2. Mix the sauce ingredients, then pour over the loaf.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until the meatloaf is no longer pink in the center.
